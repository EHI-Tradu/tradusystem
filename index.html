ABOUT

Experience built across languages, industries and borders

Evelyn Harrison Ibarra is a conference interpreter and specialized translator with more than four decades of professional experience in Venezuela and abroad. She is the founder and former director of the School of Interpreters and Translators of Caracas.

Her work combines linguistic precision, subject-matter expertise and strict confidentiality for high-level meetings, international conferences, technical projects and specialized documents.
